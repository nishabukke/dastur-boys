About Us

  • To perpetuate the memory of Shams-ul-Ulema Sardar Khan Bahadur Dr. Hoshang Jamasp Dastur, M.A., Ph.D., C.I.E., a great scholar, the Sardar Dastur Hoshang Boy's High School was inaugurated on the 8th January 1912 to meet the Educational needs of the Zoroastrians in and around Pune. Its availability as an English Medium School for boys.

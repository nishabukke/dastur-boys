PRINCIPAL's DESK

From The Principal’s Desk

‘Too often we underestimate, the power of a touch, a smile, a kind word, a listening ear, the smallest act of care, all of which have the potential to turn a life around.’

Since its inception, Sardar Dastur Hoshang Boys’ High School has steadily grown from strength to strength as we bond with every life with all its shades of requirements and needs, to be recognized as torch bearers of leading education techniques, that has students defining a future not only for themselves, but for the world at large.

2022-2023 is a significant year as the globe steps out from a virtual platform into the physical horizon, and for India in particular as we are about to witness a paradigm shift with the introduction of the National Education Policy 2020, which now places education on a pedestal, making it the sole fundamental component for achievement of full human potential to develop an equitable and just society.

We at SDHBHS firmly believe that education is the key to a nation’s progress, and with this as our goal, we take it as our bounded calling to provide the best academic and co-curricular training any student can possibly get. The school secures the students with all the necessary awareness and skills to become truly efficacious in their chosen field.

At Sardar Dastur Hoshang Boys’ High School, we ensure inclusive and equitable quality education promoting life long learning opportunities for all, which encourages critical thinking, creativity and multidisciplinarity, innovation, adaptation, and absorption of new material in novel and changing fields, where Pedagogy is evolving to make education more experiential, holistic, integrated, inquiry-driven, discovery-oriented, learner-centred, discussion-based, flexible, and most of all, enjoyable.

As the years go by, progressive changes are made, lives turn around, Sardar Dastur Hoshang Boys’ High School stands tall, towering above all, a name to be reckoned with. SDHBHS has, and will continue to meet the beliefs of the students and parents to whom we are committed. We pledge to spread the light of knowledge and message of quality education and touch many more lives and make that vital difference in this venerable institute to carve ‘Global Ready Citizens’ by being guided by the divine principle of ‘HUMATA HUKATA HUVARASHTA’ which means, ‘Good Thoughts, Good Words and Good Deeds’.

Mrs. Nisha Sahay
Principal

About Us

  • To perpetuate the memory of Shams-ul-Ulema Sardar Khan Bahadur Dr. Hoshang Jamasp Dastur, M.A., Ph.D., C.I.E., a great scholar, the Sardar Dastur Hoshang Boy's High School was inaugurated on the 8th January 1912 to meet the Educational needs of the Zoroastrians in and around Pune. Its availability as an English Medium School for boys.

The Sardar Dastur Nosherwan Girls' High School

In today's ever-changing world, one of education's great challenges is to respond to the growing demands of the education environment so as to enhance the quality of teaching and learning in schools. The new paradigm requires that the educational system adapts itself by offering an environment which adequately meets the needs of students and teachers as opposed to having school patterns fit into the existing environment. This inevitably implies flexibility and adaptations in many areas - including the planning and designing of schools which correspond to these evolving needs.

Activities

Beyond Books: Discover, Perform, Lead

Beyond academics, our school offers a dynamic range of activities to shape well-rounded individuals. From discipline-building programs like NCC and Scouts, to team spirit through sports, creativity through music, band, and choir, and future planning via career guidance sessions by Vision Verve — every student finds space to grow, lead, and express.

NCC & Scouts

Our School offers NCC, Boy Scouts & RSP activities which aim to inculcate discipline, team spirit, leadership qualities and a spirit of adventure.

Sports

The School has adequate open spaces to conduct outdoor sports like Cricket, Football, Basketball etc. In addition, the school has a Gym Hall where indoor games are organized.

Music, Band & Choir

Students are included in the School Band which displays its skills on important school functions. Selected students are also given coaching in western music to constitute the School Choir.

Career Guidance

Career guidance and counseling sessions are conducted throughout the year to help the students to identify their potential and interest in order to choose the right career.
